Demo 2
Terminal 1: Abrir simulacion
roslaunch robots_moviles turtlebot3_escenario1.launch

Terminal 2: Abrir rviz (con la configuracion guardada)
rviz -d rviz/config.rviz

Terminal 3: Teleoperacion
roslaunch turtlebot3_teleop turtlebot3_teleop_key.launch

Terminal 4: SLAM
roslaunch turtlebot3_slam turtlebot3_gmapping.launch

Terminal 5: Guardar mapa
rosrun map_server map_saver -f nombreDelMapa
